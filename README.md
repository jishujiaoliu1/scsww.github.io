Who are the people who write code for Taobao ?
==
When I was in graduate school, I was so bored after meeting the graduation requirements that I started a code writing service on Xianyu, and encountered many strange demands.The first one: A college student’s requirement is to use the target detection algorithm to detect Martian craters .When I heard this requirement, I was shocked. I never expected that the flood of deep learning has already flooded the astronomy community.After further communication, I found that there was no ready-made data and that it needed to be manually annotated. Moreover, the purpose of doing this was just to cope with a course assignment. I immediately realized that this was a laborious and unprofitable project, so I decisively told him that this requirement was too troublesome to do. He said that he could do it himself as long as I provided some consulting services.However, the result was that after asking me a bunch of basic questions, I gave up on the project and wasted several nights of my time. I earned 0 yuan.The second one: A graduate student’s requirement is to develop code that implements a certain evaluation indicator.After implementing the model as required, I found that the performance of the model did not meet his expectations. Although it was a problem with his model and not within my business scope, in order to prevent him from canceling the order directly due to dissatisfaction, I took the initiative to help him analyze the experiment, check the papers, and finally changed the evaluation index to achieve the results he wanted.I thought it would only take one night, but it ended up taking me three days, and I ended up making $298.The third one: A college student needs to complete a course assignment (implementing the code of an interpolation function)The code was ready in ten minutes, but after I sent it to him, he said it didn't run. I took a look and found that there was a problem with his Python environment. After several rounds of typing and communication without any results, I directly configured the environment for him remotely, and the program ran normally. Income 99.The fourth one: A graduate student needs to complete a course assignment (introduction to the sklearn package).He gave me a copy of the jupyter notebook code. I opened it and it seemed to be ready-made. I could just press enter to run it. The only special requirement was to add his student number somewhere in the code. The whole process was very easy and pleasant. The only unpleasant thing was that I had to help him complete a lab report.I didn't want to write lab reports for him, but he said he needed more assignments and could come to me, so I accepted the order. But he never contacted me again after the order was completed. I earned 99.Fifth: A graduate student needs to reproduce the experiment of a certain paper.When I accepted the order, he gave me a text of requirements, but I saw that it was obviously a machine translation of a paper. After I got the original paper from him, I communicated the requirements and found that he couldn't even understand the most basic concepts. In order to avoid subsequent communication, I decisively said that it couldn't be done and asked him to find someone else.Summarize:It is too troublesome to place orders online, it takes a long time, and the price that most people are willing to pay is not high, so the cost-effectiveness of this kind of order is too low.For some simple requirements, the demander is often a complete novice, and a lot of time has to be spent on communication/explanation, which is thankless and very uncomfortable to do.After these orders, in order to avoid having a cerebral hemorrhage due to various strange demands, I also gave up the code writing service.
